Roasted Brussels Sprouts

Easy, quick, healthy brussels sprouts recipe

This is a recipe for oven roasted brussels sprouts. Short, easy prep and a short cook time for brussels sprouts that come out of the oven with golden brown centers and crisp outer leaves.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds brussels sprouts
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 425°F (215°C).
  2. Halve washed and rinsed brussels sprouts.
  3. Toss brussels sprouts with 2 tablespoons of olive oil, 3/4 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon pepper.
  4. Spread brussels sprouts on baking sheet and place in oven to roast for about 20 minutes, stirring once halfway through.
  5. Remove from oven when centers are tender and golden brown.
  6. Drizzle a tablespoon of the olive oil, a tablespoon of balsamic vinegar and one teaspoon of honey then toss to coat evenly.
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning, if necessary, prior to serving.
