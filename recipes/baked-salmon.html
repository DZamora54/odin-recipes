Baked Salmon

Easy, quick, healthy baked salmon recipe

This is a recipe for oven baked salmon. Short, easy prep and a short cook time for a tasty main dish.

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Cayanne pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 425°F (215°C).
  2. Place salmon, skin side down, on baking sheet.
  3. Brush salmon with thin coat of olive oil.
  4. Season with pepper, salt, cayanne pepper to taste.
  5. Place in oven for four to six minutes per half-inch of thickness. (usually 12 to 15 minutes)
  6. Remove from oven and serve with lemon.
